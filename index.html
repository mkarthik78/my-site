Our Story

One Prime. Built on experience. Driven by purpose.

Twenty-three years. That is how long I spent inside the largest financial institutions in the country — watching retirement plan administration get treated like a commodity. A checkbox. A vendor relationship managed by contract, not by care.

I am Karthik Mohan, COO of Prime Pensions. I have managed P&L, led M&A integrations, driven technology transformation in legacy environments, and built compliance frameworks that hold up under scrutiny. What I kept seeing was the gap between what plan sponsors deserved and what they were actually getting.

Prime Pensions was built to close that gap. We operate on a simple belief: that every plan sponsor who trusts us with their employees' retirement security deserves a partner who thinks like an owner, acts with urgency, and never treats compliance as someone else's problem.

We call it One Prime — one team, one standard, one commitment. We are grateful for every client who has chosen to make this journey with us. And we are just getting started.
